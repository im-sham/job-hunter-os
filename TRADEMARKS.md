# Trademarks

`Job Hunter OS`, related logos, and any associated brand identifiers are
separate from the software license.

For current public stewardship, the project is being operated under the
`USMI Labs` umbrella. That branding context does not change the AGPL
license terms for the code.

## What The AGPL Covers

The code, templates, and other repository contents that are expressly
licensed under `AGPL-3.0-only` remain available under that software
license.

## What The AGPL Does Not Grant

The AGPL does not grant rights to use:

- the `Job Hunter OS` name as branding for a modified fork or hosted service
- project logos, icons, wordmarks, or other visual identity assets
- any branding in a way that implies endorsement, affiliation, or official status

## Allowed Nominative Use

You may use the project name to describe factual compatibility or origin,
for example:

- "Forked from Job Hunter OS"
- "Compatible with Job Hunter OS workspaces"
- "Based on the Job Hunter OS codebase"

## Forks And Hosted Derivatives

If you modify, host, or redistribute this software:

- preserve the software license and attribution notices
- mark your modifications clearly
- use your own branding for forks and hosted derivatives unless you have
  separate written permission to use the `Job Hunter OS` marks

This document is operational guidance for the public repo and is not a
statement of trademark registration status in every jurisdiction.
